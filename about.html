A B O U T M E
Head Of Software at Airntell Aerospace
Colby Pryor
Born in Ontario, Colby grew up fascinated in computers
and technology. He vividly remembers being inspired by his
father's love for electronics, which stemmed his interest in
computer science. When Colby was five, his family bought
their first desktop computer resulting in his explosion into
the realm of computing. At the age of seven, Colby said
goodbye to his Ontario friends and moved away to
Newfoundland & Labrador, where he would continue
to pursue his passion in various robotics teams along
with competitions. Colby has earned several awards for his
hard work as well as leadership in STEM-related fields,
giving him a great upper hand in post secondary.

This combined with his highly-regarded positions inside
the Sea Cadet program, molded him into an innovative leader
at Airntell Aerospace, a startup company that deploys
autonomous UAV systems for SAR operations. On top
of this, he is also a Boatswain in the Royal Canadian Naval
Reserve. A position in which he excelled in through
the Cadet program.

Colby is a dedicated individual who will use his leadership to
see any task through, no matter the scale. His work along
with mentorship has proven this countless times, making
him an effective member of any team or organization.
